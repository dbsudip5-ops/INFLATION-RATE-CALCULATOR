The annual inflation rate in Bangladesh was 8.17% in October 2025, a slight decrease from the previous month, marking the lowest rate since July 2022. This rate is a significant drop from the 9.4% in 2023 and 8.7% in 2022, but it is higher than the pre-2022 figures. 
October 2025: The annual inflation rate was 8.17%.
August 2025: The annual inflation rate was 8.29%.
2024: The end-of-period inflation rate was 10.9%.
2023: The annual inflation rate was 9.4%.
2022: The annual inflation rate was 8.7%
